THE JENDA PROJECT

DONATE

The Jenda Project is a fundraising campaign that utilizes media and fashion to bring awareness and resources to an underdeveloped primary school in rural Ethiopia. The short film that accompanies the campaign was awarded at the 2019 F.R.O. Film Festival hosted by The Center for Black Cultural and Student Affairs at the Fisher Museum. Our initial goal is to provide curriculum material, recreational equipment, as well as water security to the Jenda Primary School, and then continue building until the K-12th grade school is able to provide for all its students. There are so many ways for this to expand and keep helping people around the world but only time will tell how it manifests. Hopefully, this brand can become synonymous with black excellence and African pride.
